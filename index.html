Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a journal entry?

Tap the "+" button on the Journal tab and follow the prompts to create a new entry. You can add text, photos, voice notes, and more to document your thoughts and experiences.

How do I set a goal?

Navigate to the Goals tab, tap "Add Goal", and fill in the details for your new goal. You can set deadlines, track progress, and add notes to keep yourself accountable.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely in the cloud using Supabase's security infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Can I export my data?

Currently, we don't offer a built-in export feature. If you need to export your data, please contact our support team at support@gosloapp.ca, and we'll assist you with your request.

How do I track my progress?

GoSlo offers several ways to track your progress. View statistics in the Dashboard tab, check completed goals in the Goals section, and review past journal entries to see how far you've come.
