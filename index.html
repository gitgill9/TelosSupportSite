Frequently Asked Questions

What is Telos?

Telos is a comprehensive personal growth app that combines journaling, meditation, goal tracking, and AI coaching to help you achieve your personal goals. It's designed to support your wellbeing journey with multiple integrated tools in one seamless experience.

How do I create a journal entry?

Tap the "+" button on the Journal tab and follow the prompts to create a new entry. You can add text, photos, voice notes, and more to document your thoughts and experiences.

How do I set a goal?

Navigate to the Goals tab, tap "Add Goal", and fill in the details for your new goal. You can set deadlines, track progress, and add notes to keep yourself accountable.

What is the AI Coach feature?

The AI Coach is your personal growth companion that provides guidance, feedback, and motivation based on your journal entries and goals. It helps you gain insights, overcome challenges, and develop personalized strategies to achieve your objectives.

How do I use the AI Coach?

You can interact with the AI Coach in several ways:

  • Ask for guidance on specific goals or challenges
  • Request analysis of your journal entries for patterns and insights
  • Get personalized suggestions for meditation practices
  • Receive tailored accountability check-ins and motivation
Simply tap the AI Coach icon in the app and start a conversation about what you need help with.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take privacy and security seriously. All your personal data is stored locally on your device, not on our servers. Your journal entries, goals, and conversations with the AI Coach never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share them.

Can I export my data?

Currently, we don't offer a built-in export feature. If you need to export your data, please contact our support team at support@telosapp.ca, and we'll assist you with your request.

How do I track my progress?

Telos offers several ways to track your progress. View statistics in the Dashboard tab, check completed goals in the Goals section, and review past journal entries to see how far you've come. The AI Coach can also provide insights on your progress over time.

How does the meditation feature work?

The meditation feature offers guided and unguided sessions to help you develop mindfulness. You can select from various session lengths, background sounds, and themes. The app tracks your meditation history and helps you build a consistent practice.
