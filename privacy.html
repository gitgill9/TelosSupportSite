Thank you for choosing Telos. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our app.

Our Privacy Commitment

At Telos, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We believe your personal data belongs to you, which is why:

  • We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers
  • All your data is stored locally on your device only
  • Your journal entries, goals, and interactions with the AI Coach remain on your device
  • We have no access to your personal information
  • Your privacy is under your control at all times

Local Data Storage

The following information is stored locally on your device only:

  • User Content: Journal entries, goals, meditation logs, and AI Coach conversations
  • App Settings: Your preferences and configurations within the app
  • Usage Statistics: Basic usage data to help you track your progress

None of this information is transmitted to our servers or accessible to us unless you explicitly choose to share it (such as when contacting support).

Your Control Over Your Data

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can:

  • Delete any entries or information at any time
  • Reset the app to clear all data
  • Control app permissions through your device settings
  • Back up your data using your device's built-in backup features

App Permissions

Telos may request certain permissions on your device to provide specific features:

  • Camera/Photos: If you choose to add images to journal entries (only with your explicit permission)
  • Microphone: For voice notes functionality (only with your explicit permission)
  • Notifications: For reminders and goal tracking

These permissions are used only for providing the requested functionality and never to collect or transmit data to external servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@telosapp.ca.
